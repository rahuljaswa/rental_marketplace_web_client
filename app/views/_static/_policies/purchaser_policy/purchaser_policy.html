Operability and Safety

The Renter is responsible for using the purchased items in the manner it was designed to be used by manufacturer. The Renter should not use the items in a way that may interfere with the operability of the items or the safe operation of the item.

Item Condition and Cleanliness

The Renter should keep items in good, working condition (clean and without broken parts). BorrowBear may determine at its' sole discretion whether the purchased items is not maintained well enough by the Renter during the Purchase Period and may charge a Renter for repairs and cleaning due to a Lender’s complaint and BorrowBear’s own investigation. In the event that BorrowBear cancels a Renter’s active Purchase, BorrowBear reserves the right to also not refund any Purchase payment made by the Renter for the item.

On Time Purchases

If a Renter requests a Listing for a Purchase Period, and the Lender accepts the request, the Renter should be available to meet the Lender on-time, based on the Purchase Period that was accepted by the Lender or proposed by the Lender and accepted by the Renter.

Valuables and Property

Renters should remove all personal property that is not included in the Purchase Listing before returning the purchased items to the Lender.

Eligibility

Although it is very rare, BorrowBear may suspend or revoke a Renter’s eligibility to use BorrowBear’s services at any time for any reason, at BorrowBear's sole discretion. In the event that BorrowBear suspends or revokes a Renter, BorrowBear reserves the right to suspend or cancel any reserved or active Purchases and cancel any Purchase payments.

Damage

If, as a Renter, you notice that the purchased items was damaged during a Purchase, you must contact BorrowBear immediately and report the damage.

Theft

If, as a Renter, you believe that the Lender’s items was stolen during a Purchase, you must contact BorrowBear immediately. BorrowBear is not liable for a member’s deceptive or fraudulent acts, voluntary parting of item, and the theft of item, but we will completely cooperate with the investigation and produce information about the incident and parties involved, working with law enforcement and any insurance companies involved.

Late Returns and No Shows

If, as a Renter, you are late returning item, at an agreed upon date and time with the Lender or as specified on the Purchase transaction, you may be subject to and agree to pay any late fees to the Lender of the items as determined by the Lender. The maximum late charge per day will be the amount of a one day purchase for the Purchase listing. BorrowBear will charge these fees to your purchase payment card on file, to be paid to the Lender or may collect it using alternative billing methods. Lenders may also leave a negative review for the Renter.

If, as a Renter, you fail to show up for a scheduled Purchase Meeting, the Lender may report it to BorrowBear and leave a negative review for the Renter. Significant or multiple no shows may affect a Renter's ability to purchase items in the future, if BorrowBear believes that the no show(s) has a negative effect on the Lender and the BorrowBear community.

Accurate Member Profile

A Member, whether a Lender or Renter, must provide a completely accurate Member Profile. BorrowBear may determine at its' sole discretion whether the information on the Member’s Profile is not accurate, and may suspend the Member until the completion of BorrowBear’s investigation and conversation with the Member to resolve the issue. In the event that BorrowBear suspends a Member, BorrowBear reserves the right to suspend or cancel any reserved or active Purchases and cancel any Purchase payments.
