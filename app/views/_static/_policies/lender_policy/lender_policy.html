Lender Policy (last modified: 11/05/2016)

By signing up as a member and lending items, you agree to this Lender Policy as well as any other applicable documents, including Cancellation Policy, Renter Policy, and Web Site Terms and Privacy. The following and aforementioned policies govern your access to and use of BorrowBear, whether as a guest or a registered user. Please read this and the aforementioned policies carefully prior to creating Listings as a Lender.

Lender Fees and Payments

You will be paid the daily purchase amount you list your item for, multiplied by the length of the purchase in days.

All purchase transactions must remain in the BorrowBear system, using our infrastructure, payment, and verification systems in order to deal with fraud, customer service, verification, and other customer experience and liability related issues.

Operability and Safety

The Lender is responsible for maintaining their item in a way that is reliable and can be safely operated by the Renter. The Lender should not alter the item in a way that may interfere with the operability of the item or the safe operation of the Gear.

Item Condition

Keep your listed items in good, working condition (clean and without broken parts). Your items should be fully functional unless noted as such within the item description. You should also be the owner and in possession of your listed items. All listed items should be safe to use and/or operate and not in any way be a liability concern. BorrowBear reserves the right to determine whether an item is not in sufficiently good condition to be listed on BorrowBear apps and services. In the event that BorrowBear cancels a Lender’s active Purchase, BorrowBear reserves the right to also cancel any Purchase payments made to the Lender.

Eligibility

BorrowBear may suspend or revoke an account or specific Listing at any time, for any reason, at its' sole discretion. BorrowBear also reserves the right to suspend or cancel any reserved or active Purchases and cancel any Purchase payments.

Accurate Description

A Lender must provide a completely accurate item description, including the item's make and model, for any listed items. BorrowBear may determine at its' sole discretion whether the item description is sufficiently accurate to list. In the event that BorrowBear cancels a Lender's active Purchase, BorrowBear reserves the right to also cancel any Purchase payment made to the Lender. A Lender must upload high quality and accurate photos of any item they are listing.

On-Time Availability

If a Lender accepts a Purchase request, the Lender must make their items available for rent by the start of the purchase period.

Valuables and Property

Lenders should remove all personal property that is not included in the Purchase Listing before transferring rented items to the Renter.

Damage

If, as a Lender, you notice that your item was damaged during a Purchase, you must contact BorrowBear the same day that your item was returned by the Renter and report the damage.

Theft

If, as a Lender, you believe that your item was stolen during a Purchase, you must contact BorrowBear immediately. BorrowBear is not liable for a member’s deceptive or fraudulent acts, voluntary parting of Gear, and/or the theft of Gear, but we will completely cooperate with the investigation and produce information about the incident and parties involved, working with law enforcement and any insurance companies involved.

No Shows

If, as a Lender, you fail to show up for a schedule Purchase Meeting, the Renter may leave a negative review for the Lender and the purchase will be cancelled. Significant or multiple no shows may affect a Lender’s ability to list item in the future, if BorrowBear believes that the no show(s) has a negative effect on the Renter and the BorrowBear community.

BorrowBear as Agent

By listing your item as and Lender, you agree to appoint BorrowBear as your limited agent in matters relating your item during the listing period. These include, but are not limited to, collecting payments made by Renters on behalf of the Lender in exchange for the Purchase of the Lender’s item, providing Damage Waiver coverage, upholding and providing other guarantees and protections (if applicable), or reporting the Lender's item as stolen if BorrowBear believes it is so in good faith.

Accurate Member Profile

A Member, where a Lender or Renter, must provide a completely accurate Member Profile. BorrowBear may determine at its' sole discretion whether the information on the Member’s Profile is not accurate, and may suspend the Member until the completion of BorrowBear’s investigation and conversation with the Member to resolve the issue. In the event that BorrowBear suspends a Member, BorrowBear reserves the right to suspend or cancel any reserved or active Purchases and cancel any Purchase payments.

You as a Lender understand and agree that you are solely responsible for determining (i) your applicable Tax reporting requirements, and (ii) the Taxes that should be included in a Listing price, and for including Taxes to be collected or obligations relating to applicable Taxes in your Listings.

You understand and acknowledge that appropriate governmental agencies, departments or authorities (the "Tax Authority") where you are located may require Taxes to be collected from Lenders and Renters on the amount paid for the right to use of Gear, and to be remitted to the respective Tax Authority.
