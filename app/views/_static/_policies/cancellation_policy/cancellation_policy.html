Cancellation Policy (last modified: 11/05/2016)

Renter Cancellation

A Renter may cancel a rental request at their discretion any time before a rental request has been accepted. However, if a Lender has already accepted and confirmed the rental request, the Renter must cancel the rental request 24 hours prior to the start of the Rental Period in Greenwich Mean Time. Frequent or multiple cancellations will affect the Renter's reputation on BorrowBear and BorrowBear reserves the right to restrict or delete the Renter's account. Cancellations that occur less than 24 hours before the Rental Period start date may incur a 100% charge to the renter at (no refund of the total rental charge) BorrowBear's and the owner's discretion.

Lender Cancellation

A Lender may remove a listing or cancel a Rental Request at their discretion. However, if the Owner has already accepted and confirmed the rental, they must cancel the request 24 hours prior to the start of the Rental Period in Greenwich Mean Time. Frequent or multiple cancellations will affect the Lender's reputation on BorrowBear and BorrowBear reserves the right to restrict or delete the Lender's account.
