Help

What is BorrowBear?

BorrowBear is a marketplace that provides a safe and transparent way for people to rent things from each other.

Why should I list my items on BorrowBear?

In our experience, almost everyone in the world owns possessions that could be earning income. If you are interested in making up to $1,000 per week using items that you already own, BorrowBear is for you.

How much of a fee does take BorrowBear take?

You receive 100% of the price, multiplied by the length of the rental, that you list your item for. We add minor platform and transaction fees that the renter pays.

What happens if something goes wrong with my rental?

We collect a security deposit when a rental is confirmed by both parties and the renter agrees to a standard rental agreement with the person providing the item. In case there are any issues, BorrowBear will arbitrate the dispute and provide the owner of the item the fair value of their damages. In most cases, the owner of the product will receive the full replacement cost of the item. Our goal is to provide an attractive platform policy for both owners and renters.

What is your cancellation policy?

We have a very flexible cancellation policy. Either the owner or the renter can cancel a rental until the rental date begins. If you have any issues after that, we can handle your requests on a case-by-case basis. Please send an email to support@borrowbear.com

When will my credit card by charged?

If you are renting a product within the next 20 days, your credit card will be charged immediately. If you are renting a product more than 20 days into the future, you will be charged 20 days before the rental date.

When will I receive payment from the renter?

If you are a product owner renting your product, we will send a transfer to your bank account on file 1 day after the starting rental date. If you do not yet have an account on file by the rental date, you will automatically receive your payment shortly after entering your account information.

How can I contact BorrowBear for support?

You can always contact us at support@borrowbear.com.

Will the renter know who I am?

The renter will only be able to see your username. If you use an unrevealing username, the renter will not know your identity unless you disclose it. In general, we strongly recommend not sharing personal details or addresses with renters. Setting a public pickup location (for example, the Starbucks down the street) is a strongly recommended best practice. In certain areas, we do not allow users to post a personal address.

How do I know the buyer or renter is a real person?

Everyone who lists or rents on BorrowBear has to verify their phone number and email address. Moreover, they must provide unique identification information (e.g. passport or drivers' license) to receive any payments.

Can I use BorrowBear to advertise my services online?

Yes, you can list anything you want that is legal and in good taste. We just think it works really well for product rentals.
